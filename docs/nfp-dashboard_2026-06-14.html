JOLTS-implied net NFP: Hires (5.1M) − Separations (5.0M) = +100K/month implied by flow data — below the 130–214K range currently reported in 2026. The gap is consistent with ~50–100K B-D model additions per month inflating the CES stock-change estimate.

Low quits rate (1.9%): Workers are not voluntarily leaving for better jobs. This is structurally inconsistent with a labor market generating genuine +172K/month strength. It signals a "slow-hire, slow-fire" equilibrium, not expansion.
